# Support Contacts & Information

Welcome to the **My Wealth Tracker** support page. If you are experiencing issues or have feedback, please review the resources below or contact us directly.

## 1. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### I lost my device or deleted the app. Can you recover my data?
Unfortunately, **no**. As stated in our Privacy Policy, we do not store your data on external servers. If you did not enable iCloud Sync and do not have an iOS device backup, your data is permanently lost.

### How do I report a bug?
If you've found a bug, please include the following details in your email to help us investigate:
1. Version of My Wealth Tracker you are using (found in Settings -> About).
2. iOS Version (e.g., iOS 17.4) and Device Model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro).
3. Steps to reproduce the bug.
4. Screenshots/Screen recordings.

### How can I suggest a feature?
We love hearing feature requests from our community! Please use the subject line "Feature Request:" and include your idea. We prioritize development based on user demand.
