# Wealth - All In One Tracker - User Guide

## Welcome to Wealth App
*Your complete personal finance dashboard*

- **Overview:** Dashboard with customizable widgets
- **Budget:** Allocate income and track what's left
- **Net Wealth:** Monitor all your assets in one place
- **Debt:** Track and plan debt payoff
- **Subscriptions:** Manage recurring costs and renewals

## Budget
*Allocate income and track what's left*

- **Groups:** Organize categories into budget groups
- **Categories:** Set up income sources and expense categories
- **Allocations:** Assign amounts and track remaining balance
- **Progress:** See budget utilization at a glance
- **CSV Import:** Bulk import budget categories

## Net Wealth
*Track your complete financial picture*

- **All Asset Types:** Cash, stocks, crypto, property, and more
- **Crypto Wallets:** Auto-track 8 blockchains and 3 exchanges
- **Live Prices:** Real-time crypto and stock pricing
- **Snapshots:** Track net worth over time
- **CSV Import:** Bulk import assets from spreadsheets

## Debt Management
*Plan your path to debt freedom*

- **Track Debts:** Add all your debts with rates and payments
- **Payoff Progress:** Visualize your payoff journey
- **Timeline:** See estimated payoff dates
- **CSV Import:** Bulk import debts from spreadsheets

## Subscriptions
*Never lose track of recurring costs*

- **Track All Subs:** Monthly, yearly, weekly billing cycles
- **Reminders:** Get notified before renewals
- **Cost Analysis:** See total monthly and yearly costs
- **CSV Import:** Bulk import subscriptions

## Overview Dashboard
*43 customizable widgets at your fingertips*

- **Charts:** Interactive pie charts and bar graphs
- **FIRE Progress:** Track financial independence goals
- **Goals:** Monitor savings and financial goals
- **Customize:** Drag, drop, and toggle widgets

## Settings
*Personalize your experience*

- **25 Themes:** Multiple color themes to match your style
- **150+ Currencies:** Track wealth in any world currency
- **Sign in with Apple:** Secure authentication with your Apple ID
- **iCloud Sync:** Keep your data in sync across devices
- **Security:** Face ID, Touch ID, and PIN protection
- **Audit Logs:** All actions logged for 15 days — view in Advanced Settings

## Reports & Backup
*Export, analyze, and protect your data*

- **22 Report Types:** PDF and CSV reports for every aspect
- **CSV Export:** Export your data to spreadsheets
- **Sample Reports:** Preview reports before generating
- **Backup & Restore:** Scheduled or on-demand backups

## Your Privacy
*Your data stays yours — always*

- **On-Device Only:** All data is stored locally on your device
- **Encrypted Storage:** Your financial data is encrypted at rest
- **No Data Collection:** We never access, collect, or share your data
- **No External Servers:** No accounts, no cloud tracking, no analytics
- **No Third-Party Access:** Your information is never sold or shared
