# Privacy Policy for Wealth

**Last Updated:** 13th, Mar 2026

At My Wealth Tracker, your privacy is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your data.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, access, or share your personal data.** 
Wealth App is designed to operate completely offline as a "local-first" application. 
- **No External Servers:** We do not maintain user accounts, perform cloud tracking, or send your data to any external analytics servers.
- **On-Device Processing:** All of your financial information and app usage data are stored and processed locally on your device.

## 2. Data Storage and Security
- **Encrypted Storage:** Your financial data is securely encrypted at rest on your device.
- **iCloud Sync:** If you enable iCloud sync to keep your data synchronized across your Apple devices, your data is securely stored within your private iCloud account via Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure. We do not have access to your iCloud data.
- **Biometric Security:** We offer the ability to secure the app via Face ID, Touch ID, or PIN protection. These authentication methods are processed natively by your Apple device and your biometric data is never shared.

## 3. Third-Party Access
**None.** Because all data is stored locally on your device, we do not share, sell, or rent any of your information to third parties. 

## 4. Third-Party Services
If you interact with third-party tools via the application (e.g., fetching live crypto/stock pricing), the app communicates directly with these public APIs. No personal or identifiable information is transmitted in these requests.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and, if the changes are significant, we may provide a more prominent notice within the app.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us. Please reference the Support Contacts document for how to reach out.
